Adventure Wagon - Van and camper builds

The Adventure Wagon Configurator empowers customers to craft their ideal camper van, tailoring every detail to match their distinct desires and requirements. Within this innovative program, an array of customization possibilities awaits, encompassing choices like bed dimensions, interior layout, storage setups, and even the inclusion of amenities such as sinks, stoves, or refrigerators. Furthermore, customers are granted the freedom to select from a diverse palette of finishes and materials, enabling them to imbue their conversion kit with a personalized aesthetic that suits their individual style.

Cool thing is that Arne and I would like to build a van like this ourselves, which makes it an ideal tool to create a design and start building!

Nike By You

The Nike By You shoe configurator is an innovative and interactive online tool offered by Nike that allows customers to personalize and customize their own pair of Nike shoes. This platform is designed to provide customers with a unique and engaging shopping experience, where they can take an active role in designing their footwear.

I'm someone who loves sneakers and I've used this tool a couple of times to create my own pair of shoes. It's a fun and interactive way to create a unique pair of shoes that no one else has! And it has my name on it, which is pretty cool!
